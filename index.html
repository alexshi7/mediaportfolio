About

Photography, videography
and editing

From a young age, I've enjoyed the artistic freedom and expressive power of media. I pursue photography and videography in my freetime, photographing dancers and musicians in performance. My work focuses on rapid-burst sequences that reveal movement as a layered narrative rather than a single frozen moment.

Editor: Jason Bak specializes in photo editing and post-production, with a keen eye for detail and a passion for enhancing visual storytelling. Jason has experience in Adobe Photoshop, Lightroom, and Premiere Pro. Together, we have collaborated on various projects, combining our skills to create compelling visual narratives that have earned recognition from the Cornell Cinema Advisory Board. Our Instagram @berkeliumarsenide features our latest work. We are available for concert series, rehearsal shoots, and editorial work.
